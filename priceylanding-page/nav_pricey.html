    Navigating Pricey

    Pricey's interface is divided into 4 areas:

    • Header - contains the actions of the website, Home, products, contacts, about and FAQs and your profile.
    • Sidebar - Filter search and subscribe to pricey’s newsletter
    • Main pane - displays a list of the available categories, top deals,top products, contact us,store directory and top brands.
    • Footer - contains the links to pricey’s social media, list your online shop, download the pricey app and many more.

    Header

    The header allows you to switch between Five different views

    • Get Access to Pricey’s home page which is the page the users will see first.
    • Get Access to pricey’s Product page by clicking on “Product”, this page highlights all the products available on the pricey website.
    • Contact our Pricey team to file complaint and more by clicking on “contact”
    • Get to know more about Pricey from the about page
    • Access your profile

    Sidebar

    From the sidebar, you can:

    • Filter you search according to most popular category, price range and the color you would prefer
    • Get Access to pricey’s news letter by inputting a valid email address and get notified about trending products and products with the best available deals weekly.

    Pricey tip: You can move the “slider” back and forth to adjust the price range.

    Main Pane

    From the Main pane, you can:

    • Search for an item using the search bar provided
    • See all the categories offered on the pricey website
    • Access the pricey “top deals”, top deals are updated every 48 hrs on the pricey website
    • Access the pricey “top products”, these are products that are mostly searched by users on the pricey website
    • Contact the pricey team for complaints, feedback and more.
    • See latest pricey news
    • View top brands working pricey.
    • Access store directory, this leads to the home page of the pricey brand stores

    Footer

    From the footer, you can:

    • “Browse prices” of different items under various categories.
    • Learn more about pricey through the “about us”, see frequently asked questions from pricey users, view latest pricey news and contact the pricey team.
    • Log in to your pricey account
    • Become a merchant on pricey with ease by clicking on the “List your online shop”
    • See online shops working with pricey
    • Subscribe to the pricey weekly Newsletter.
    • Get a link to download the pricey app from google play store and App store.
    • Get a link to pricey social media accounts and get daily updates on top deals and promo deals.
